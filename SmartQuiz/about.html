About Us


Free Quiz Hosting

         We can host your online Quiz contest on Quizzer at no cost. The entire hosting and infrastructure is taken care by us. The Quiz can accept any skills MCQs and support any number of Users.

Quiz Creation

         With an Easy User Interface and few simple clicks, you can set up a Quiz in no time. Contestants can particpate from any where and any device. We support Public and Private contests including Secured Quizzes. Hassle free process which saves effort and time for the Organizers.

Scalability and Leaderboard
